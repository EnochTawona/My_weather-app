Explore North-Western Province's Tourism

The North-Western Province of Zambia offers a diverse range of tourist attractions, from breathtaking natural wonders to rich cultural heritage sites. Explore our national parks, game reserves, waterfalls, and cultural villages to experience the ultimate African adventure.

National Parks and Game Reserves

Our national parks and game reserves are home to an abundance of wildlife, including lions, leopards, elephants, and buffaloes. Visit Liuwa Plains National Park and West Lunga National Park for an unforgettable safari experience.

Waterfalls

The North-Western Province is home to some of Zambia's most spectacular waterfalls, including Kundalila Falls. Take a hike through the lush surroundings and marvel at the natural beauty.

Visit Mukuni Village and experience the rich cultural heritage of the North-Western Province. Learn about the traditions and customs of the local people.
